We are conducting a study about brand perception that is reflected in discussion on Twitter. This study focuses in particular on a telecommunications company, Orange, which is a branch of France Telecom.

You will be presented with a series of tweets, each followed by several questions that aim to assess the relevance of the tweet and how it reflects the Twitter user's sentiment.

About us: We are a research group at USC's AIL and SAIL labs, comprised of social scientists and engineers. We proudly support open data and research, including open source software and public dissemination of our results.

Note to Mechanical Turkers: We verify the quality of the results using randomly presented indicator stimuli. However, we select Turkers with high past acceptance, so in practice we reject very few HITs.
